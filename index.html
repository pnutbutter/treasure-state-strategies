Why us?

We believe in serving our clients by working hard, building relationships and getting results - with respect and trust. While the national scene becomes ever more politically divisive, we strive to represent Montana's values and way of life - finding common ground, having civil discourse, and finding solutions to complex issues that affect business in our state.

Montana is a handshake state - and at Treasure State Solutions, we believe that reputation matters. We deliver our clients professional services while advancing their interests and holding ourselves to the highest standards of ethics and integrity.

As a boutique lobbying firm, Treasure State Solutions also sets itself apart by our level of specialization and customer service. We have a strict conflict of interest policy to ensure our client's needs are always our top priority.

OUR AMAZING TEAM

Kali Wicks

Government Relations Specialist

Kali Wicks was raised on a dryland wheat farm in northcentral Montana and went on to attain an undergraduate degree in Political Science and a Master's in Communications and Organizational Leadership from Gonzaga University. Kali also graduated with honors from the Masters of Public Administration program at the University of Montana. Kali's key values include trust, integrity, and relationships.

Prior to Treasure State Strategies, Kali spent a decade working in Government Relations for the largest health insurance company in the state. This work included expertise in a multitude of areas across the spectrum of public affairs including building strong, long-lasting relationships on both sides of the aisle across several administrations, developing and executing plans to pass or defeat legislation, writing legislation, direct lobbying, performing grassroots and issue advocacy, coalition building, regulatory relations, tribal relations and managing a federal PAC. Kali also worked closely with related health plans in several states on joint initiatives and legislative campaigns and advised the organization's D.C. office on state and federal matters relating to rural health and business issues. Kali also has experience working in housing and community development, workforce development and higher education.

Kali is a strong proponent of servant leadership and giving back to Montana communities and organizations. She is currently the chair of the Montana HOSA-Future Health Professionals Advisory Board and serves on the MPA Program Advisory Board at the Alexander Blewitt III Law School in Missoula, on the board for the Awareness Network and is a member of the MBAC (Montana Business Assistance Connection) Board. Kali has previously served as the president of the Helena Symphony Board, the advisory board for the Women's Foundation of Montana, and the executive board for the Career Training Institute in Helena. Kali has also played a large role both fundraising and teaching Lobbying 101 at the Women's NEW Leadership project - a week-long summer experience for college age women designed to provide leadership and policy experience. Kali has also presented guest lectures on lobbying and policy work at Carroll College and the University of Montana.

In her free time, you can find Kali on a Montana adventure with family and friends. Whether she's camping, exploring ghost towns, helping out on the family farm or visiting the many parks and public lands Montana has to offer - Kali believes in enjoying all our great state has to offer.
